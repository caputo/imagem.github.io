We breathe geo

Geolocation is at heart of every piece of software produced by Imagem. Here you'll find information about some projects and utilities that we built and use to empower our applications with geolocation capabilities. Fell free to use it and if you think you could help us on improving it, just drop us a pull request. We'll be glad to see what are you doing to empower geo-apps!

fc2json

When querying a esri feature service to get all data published through it, it is needed to deal with the limitation on the number of records returned by the server. fc2json abstracts the tasks needed to get all data from a esri feature service.
